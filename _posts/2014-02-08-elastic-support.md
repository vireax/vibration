## Finite element method ##

Stiffness of member 'e'

<div> $$ k^{(e)}  = \frac{AE}{l} \begin{bmatrix}1 & -1 \\-1 & 1 \end{bmatrix} $$ </div>

Member stiffness in global coordinate

<div> $$ \underline{k} =  \underline{T}^{T} \hat{k} \underline{T } $$ </div>
